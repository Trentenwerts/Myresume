Awards and Achievements

Being a member of the Boy Scouts of America taught me many skills and life lessons. Becoming an eagle scout is a big deal as only 2% of all Boy Scouts become one. During my scounting I completed 20+ merit badges and provided 40+ hours of service.

Best Teammate Award

I recieved this award while playing baseball at Corner Canyon Highschool. I recieved this award through vote from my teammates, coaches, and administrators. I showed my team that I was a team player and that I was able to work well as a team.
